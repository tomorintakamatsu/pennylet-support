Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?

No. PennyLet is designed to work locally on your iPhone. You do not need an account to use the app.

Where is my budgeting data stored?

Your budgets, transactions, goals, categories, and app settings are stored on your device. Because the app is local-first, deleting the app or resetting app data can remove local data, so export a CSV first if you want a backup.

How do I export or delete my data?

Use the export and reset tools inside PennyLet settings. You can also delete the app from your iPhone to remove local app data from the device.

How do I cancel PennyLet Pro?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings, tap your Apple ID, open Subscriptions, select PennyLet, and choose Cancel Subscription.

Does PennyLet link to my bank?

No. PennyLet does not require a bank connection. You can add transactions manually, import CSV data, and use receipt scanning if you choose.

How does receipt scanning work?

When you choose to scan a receipt, PennyLet asks for camera access. You can review extracted details before saving a transaction.

How do AI insights work?

AI features are optional. When you request an AI insight or receipt extraction, the app may send the information needed for that request to an AI processing service so it can return the result.
