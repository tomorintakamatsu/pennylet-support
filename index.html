Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?
PennyLet uses Base44 for authentication. Your credentials are managed securely through Base44.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, select PennyLet, and tap Cancel Subscription.

How is my data stored?
Your financial data is stored securely on Base44 servers. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
